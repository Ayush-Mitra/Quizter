About This Project

Welcome to our PDF-Based Quiz Generator, an innovative tool designed to transform educational and informational PDFs into engaging multiple-choice quizzes — instantly and intelligently.

Whether you're a teacher, student, trainer, or self-learner, this platform helps reinforce learning by converting raw content into interactive assessments. Powered by the PDF.js library for efficient text extraction and smart keyword algorithms, our system analyzes uploaded PDFs to generate meaningful MCQs, complete with distractors and a fill-in-the-blank question format.

Once the quiz is generated, users can:


Interactively attempt questions
Get instant feedback
View performance through a real-time result chart

This project emphasizes:


Automation: No manual question-making — just upload and go.
Accessibility: No logins, no setup — works in-browser.
Visualization: Get a clear overview of your performance with a color-coded doughnut chart.

It's more than just a quiz — it's a step toward smarter, more accessible learning.
